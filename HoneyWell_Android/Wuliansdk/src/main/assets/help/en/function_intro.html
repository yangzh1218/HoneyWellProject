Add device

Log on the client into my device page,click on the top right corner of the add icon for scanning the qr code at the bottom of the device.Please follow the screen prompts to complete the add device and WiFi connection.

Live video

After adding device,click online device in my equipment list for immediate viewing live video.Live at the interface can also pause / play,mute,full screen.

Device management

After the success of adding device,can be related to the operation of device.Selecting a device in my device list to click on the icon set device,which can carry out actions such as modifying the device name,replaced set the device off the reminders,WIFI replacement,motion detection settings;you can also delete a device,authorized device.

Motion detection

Selecting a device,In my list of devices for accessing device management interface."Mobile detection"can set the security protection enable / disable,safety protection plan time setting,sensitivity setting,the mobile regional settings in the monitoring range.
