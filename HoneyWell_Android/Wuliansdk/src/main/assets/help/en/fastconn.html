Honeywell smart cameras support most of the routers in the market (except enterprise routers), and the common settings are as follows:

1. Router encryption mode is WPA/WPA2;

2. Close the AP Isolation in the router settings;

3. Turn off the wireless visit; cancel the black and white list options;

4. Open DHCP.

The following operations can avoid the failure of networking:

1. Avoid the use of portable WiFi;

2. When using a dual-band router, please make the device connect to 2.4G band WiFi;

3. Suggested using non-bridging WiFi
